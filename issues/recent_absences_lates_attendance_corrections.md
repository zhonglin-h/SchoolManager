### Feature Design: Recent Absences, Lates, and Attendance Corrections

**Main Goal:**
- Provide the principal with a centralized page to quickly update themselves on recent absences and late records.
- Allow modifying or correcting past attendance records for any specified time period.

**Requirements:**
1. **Scope:**
   - Display and manage absences, lates, and other attendance enums.
   - Filter presets: "past day," "past week," and a custom time period in days.
   - Default records displayed: Absences and Lates.

2. **Layout:**
   - Tabular layout for organizing data.

3. **Data Display:**
   - Show: Student Name, Date/Time, Attendance Type (e.g., Late, Absent), Reason (if available), and Notes (optional).
   - Include pagination for large datasets.

4. **Filtering Options:**
   - Filters: Date Range, Attendance Type (Absences/Lates/Present), and Student Name.

5. **Editing Feature:**
   - Allow editing records to update Type, Date, Reason, and Notes directly from the table.

**Suggestions:**
Include ideas such as audit trails, quick actions for reviewing records, summary statistics, export options (CSV), and color indicators for easy identification of records.
